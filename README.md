This dashboard highlights important trends in sales and profitability across products, customers, and regions. Overall, the dashboard helps focus attention on profitable products, high-performing regions, and areas needing strategic improvement

üìä Key Insights

a) Categories and sub-categories contribute differently to overall sales and profit, highlighting clear top and bottom performers.
b) Certain customer segments generate high sales but lower profit, indicating the need for better pricing or discount strategies.
c) Discount levels have a direct impact on profitability, with some high-discount products consistently resulting in losses.
d) Sales and profit trends over time reveal seasonal patterns and periods of strong business growth.
e) Regional and state-level analysis shows uneven profitability, helping identify high-performing and underperforming areas.
f) A small group of products drives a significant portion of revenue, while a few products negatively impact overall profit.

üí° Key Business Recommendations

a) Review and optimize discount strategies for products that consistently generate low or negative profit.
b) Focus marketing and inventory efforts on high-performing categories and sub-categories that drive maximum revenue.
c) Re-evaluate pricing and cost structures for customer segments with high sales but low profitability.
d) Strengthen presence in high-profit regions and analyze underperforming states to identify improvement opportunities.
* Leverage seasonal sales trends to plan promotions, inventory, and staffing more effectively.
* Consider discontinuing or redesigning products that consistently underperform in both sales and profit.
